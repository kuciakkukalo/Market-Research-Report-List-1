<p><p>The Pedestrians Automated Emergency Braking (AEB) system market encompasses three key types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning alerts drivers to potential collisions with pedestrians, allowing them to react. Dynamic Brake Support enhances braking efforts when a collision is likely. Crash Imminent Braking automatically engages the brakes to prevent or mitigate an accident if the driver fails to respond in time. Together, these technologies aim to enhance pedestrian safety and reduce accidents.</p></p>
