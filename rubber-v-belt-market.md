<p><p>The Rubber V-belt market is primarily categorized into two types: Wrapped V-belts and Cut V-belts. Wrapped V-belts feature a layer of fabric around the rubber, enhancing their resistance to wear and environmental factors, making them suitable for general-purpose applications. In contrast, Cut V-belts are produced from raw rubber without a protective covering, offering flexibility and custom lengths for specific machinery needs. Each type serves distinct operational requirements, catering to various industries including automotive, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
