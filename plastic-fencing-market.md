<p><ul><li>Vinyl</li><li>HDPE</li><li>Wood-plastic Composites (WPC)</li><li>Polystyrene</li><li>Polyethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic fencing market comprises several types, each with unique characteristics. Vinyl fencing is popular for its durability and low maintenance. High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) offers resistance to UV radiation and impact, making it a robust choice. Wood-Plastic Composites (WPC) blend wood fibers with plastics for a natural appearance while resisting rot. Polystyrene is lightweight and affordable, ideal for decorative applications. Polyethylene is flexible and resistant to moisture, commonly used for temporary and permanent fencing solutions.</p></p>
