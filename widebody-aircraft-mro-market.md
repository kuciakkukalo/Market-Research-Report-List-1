Airframe and Modification MRO involves the structural maintenance and upgrades of the aircraft's body. Line Maintenance is routine upkeep conducted between flights, addressing minor repairs and inspections. Component MRO covers the maintenance of individual aircraft components, such as landing gear and avionics, ensuring their reliability and compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
