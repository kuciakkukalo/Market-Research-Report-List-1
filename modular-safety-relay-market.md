<p><ul><li>Single Channel Modular Safety Relay</li><li>Dual Channel Modular Safety Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Modular Safety Relay market includes Single Channel and Dual Channel systems, each designed for safety applications in industrial settings. Single Channel Modular Safety Relays utilize one circuit to monitor safety devices, offering a cost-effective solution for less complex safety requirements. In contrast, Dual Channel Modular Safety Relays employ two independent circuits, enhancing reliability and fault tolerance by cross-monitoring safety inputs. This redundancy makes them ideal for applications where higher safety standards are mandated, ensuring robust hazard management.</p></p>
