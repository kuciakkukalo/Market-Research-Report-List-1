<p><ul><li>Belt Drive Openers</li><li>Chain Drive Openers</li><li>Screw Drive Openers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart garage door openers market is primarily categorized into three types: belt drive, chain drive, and screw drive openers. Belt drive openers are known for their quiet operation, ideal for homes adjacent to living spaces. Chain drive openers are robust and economical, making them suitable for heavy doors but can be noisier. Screw drive openers use a threaded steel rod for movement, offering fewer moving parts for lower maintenance but may be affected by temperature fluctuations. Each type caters to specific consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
