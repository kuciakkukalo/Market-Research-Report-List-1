<p><p>The Telescope Laser Telemeter market includes two primary types: Monocular and Multicular Telescope types. Monocular telescopes are compact, featuring a single optical tube, ideal for portable applications and quick measurements in various environments. In contrast, Multicular telescopes utilize multiple optical tubes, offering enhanced magnification and a broader field of view, suitable for detailed long-distance measurements. Both types cater to diverse industries such as surveying, construction, and military, providing precision and efficiency in distance measurement tasks.</p></p>
