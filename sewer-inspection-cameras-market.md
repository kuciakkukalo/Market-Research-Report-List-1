<p><strong>The Sewer Inspection Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push Rod Type</li><li>Crawler Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sewer inspection cameras market comprises two main types: push rod type and crawler type. Push rod cameras consist of a flexible rod with a camera at the end, allowing for easy navigation through straight pipelines and minor bends. They are ideal for smaller or less complex systems. Crawler type cameras feature robotic vehicles that can traverse larger pipes, navigate bends, and provide high-resolution imaging. These are suitable for extensive sewer systems, offering detailed inspections over longer distances.</p></p>
