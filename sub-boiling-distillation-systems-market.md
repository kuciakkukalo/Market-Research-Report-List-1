<p><p>Sub-boiling distillation systems are designed for applications requiring precise control of temperature and purity. The Single Quartz system offers a straightforward design for basic separation tasks, using a single layer of quartz glass for efficient heat retention and minimal contamination. In contrast, the Double Quartz system enhances performance through a dual-layer design, providing improved thermal insulation and stability. This results in superior purity levels and optimized separation processes, making both systems essential in fields such as pharmaceuticals and analytical chemistry.</p></p>
