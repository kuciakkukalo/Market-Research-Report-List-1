<p><p>Flat engines are a type of reciprocating engine where cylinders are arranged horizontally, enhancing stability and lowering the center of gravity. Flat-six (6 cylinders) engines provide smooth power delivery, found in many high-performance cars, especially Porsches. Larger configurations include flat-eight, flat-ten, flat-twelve, and flat-sixteen engines, which deliver immense power but are less common due to size and complexities. Other variations may include different configurations or hybrid designs.</p></p>
