<p><p>The thermal plasterboard market is primarily segmented into tapered-edged and square-edged boards. Tapered-edged plasterboards feature beveled edges that allow for seamless joints, making them ideal for modern finishing techniques and ensuring a smooth surface for painting or tiling. Square-edged boards, on the other hand, provide a straightforward installation with a flat edge, suitable for traditional applications. Both types offer thermal insulation benefits, enhancing energy efficiency in buildings while contributing to improved comfort and reduced heating costs.</p></p>
