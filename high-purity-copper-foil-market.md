<p><p>The High Purity Copper Foil market is primarily segmented into two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through mechanical rolling processes, offering superior physical properties and typically used in applications like flexible printed circuits and batteries. Electrolytic copper foil, on the other hand, is generated through an electrochemical process, resulting in thinner and more uniform sheets, ideal for high-density applications such as electric vehicles and advanced electronics, emphasizing conductivity and durability.</p></p>
