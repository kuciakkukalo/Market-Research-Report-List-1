<p><p>The neurosurgical drainage devices market comprises primarily shunt types and anti-siphon devices. Shunt types include ventricular shunts that manage cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) flow, helping treat hydrocephalus by diverting excess fluid. Anti-siphon devices prevent excessive drainage of CSF due to pressure changes, reducing complications like over-drainage. These devices are crucial in neurosurgery, enhancing patient outcomes by ensuring proper CSF management and minimizing symptoms related to neurological disorders, ultimately improving safety and quality of life for patients.</p></p>
