<p><p>The Dedicated Energy Metering IC Chip market is categorized into two main types: Simplex and Polyphase. Simplex metering ICs measure energy consumption for single-phase systems, making them ideal for residential applications. In contrast, Polyphase metering ICs are designed for three-phase systems, commonly used in industrial and commercial settings. Each type offers specific functionalities tailored to their respective applications, with simplex chips focusing on simplicity and efficiency, while polyphase chips provide advanced measurement capabilities for more complex energy management needs.</p></p>
