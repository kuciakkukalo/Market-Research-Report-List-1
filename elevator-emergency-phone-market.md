<p><p>The Elevator Emergency Phone market comprises two primary types: wireless and wired elevator phones. Wireless elevator phones utilize cellular or radio technology, offering flexibility in installation without extensive wiring, making them suitable for retrofitting in existing buildings. In contrast, wired elevator phones connect directly to a telephone line, ensuring reliable communication during emergencies. Both types serve crucial safety functions, providing immediate access to emergency services, but their choice depends on factors like building infrastructure, budget, and specific safety requirements.</p></p>
