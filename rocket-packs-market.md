<p><p>The rocket packs market is categorized into two main types based on propellant state: gases and liquids. Gas-based rocket packs utilize pressurized gases for propulsion, often resulting in simpler systems with quicker deployments, while liquid rocket packs offer higher efficiency and greater thrust through the combination of liquid propellants. While gas packs are typically suited for short-duration flights and smaller payloads, liquid packs cater to advanced applications, enabling longer missions and heavier payload capacities, thus enhancing their operational versatility.</p></p>
