<p><p>Photorejuvenation equipment encompasses various technologies used for skin treatment, each utilizing different light modalities. Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs) are used for their skin healing and anti-aging properties, emitting low-intensity light. Lasers deliver targeted energy to improve skin texture and tone, effectively treating specific issues. Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) emits broad-spectrum light, addressing pigmentation and vascular lesions. Other thermal methods, such as radiofrequency and ultrasound, promote collagen production and tighten skin, enhancing overall appearance and texture through heat application.</p></p>
