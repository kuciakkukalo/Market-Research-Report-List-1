<p><p>The single-use bioprocessor sensor market primarily includes optical dissolved oxygen sensors and pH sensors. Optical dissolved oxygen sensors use fluorescence technology to measure oxygen levels in bioprocesses, ensuring optimal conditions for microbial growth. pH sensors, on the other hand, monitor the acidity or alkalinity of solutions, which is critical for maintaining the quality and efficiency of biopharmaceutical production. Both types enhance process control, reduce contamination risks, and facilitate efficient monitoring in single-use bioreactors.</p></p>
