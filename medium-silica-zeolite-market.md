<p><p>The Medium Silica Zeolite market comprises two primary types: natural zeolites and synthetic zeolites. Natural zeolites are naturally occurring minerals formed through volcanic activity, valued for their ion-exchange and adsorption properties. They are often used in agriculture and environmental applications. Synthetic zeolites, on the other hand, are manufactured through hydrothermal processes, allowing for tailored properties and greater purity. These synthesized materials find applications in catalysis, detergents, and gas separation, catering to diverse industrial needs and enhancing performance.</p></p>
