<p><p>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCOS) technology is a versatile display method used in various volumetric display types. Swept volume volumetric displays project 3D images by rapidly sweeping a light source across a space. Static volume volumetric displays create 3D effects through fixed light patterns in a defined volume. Multiplanar volumetric displays utilize multiple 2D layers to construct a 3D image, allowing for varying perspectives. These distinct approaches cater to different applications, enhancing visualization in fields like medicine, entertainment, and design.</p></p>
