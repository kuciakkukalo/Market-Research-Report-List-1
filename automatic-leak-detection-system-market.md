<p><p>The Automatic Leak Detection System market comprises two primary types: Fully Automatic and Semi-Automatic systems. Fully Automatic Leak Detection Systems operate independently, utilizing advanced sensors and algorithms to identify and report leaks in real-time without human intervention. In contrast, Semi-Automatic Leak Detection Systems require some level of manual input for monitoring and analysis, providing alerts when issues are detected but relying on operators to confirm and act on the findings. Both systems enhance safety and efficiency in leak management.</p></p>
