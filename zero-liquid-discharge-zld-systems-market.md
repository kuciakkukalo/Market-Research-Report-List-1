<p><p>Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) Systems are integrated approaches aimed at minimizing wastewater discharge by recovering and reusing water to achieve zero liquid effluent. The growing need for sustainable practices, combined with the scarcity of fresh water resources, has catalyzed the adoption of ZLD technologies across various sectors.</p><p>The market for ZLD systems is witnessing significant growth, driven by rising awareness of environmental sustainability and the necessity of water conservation.
