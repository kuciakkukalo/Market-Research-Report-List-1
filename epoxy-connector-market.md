<p><p>The epoxy connector market is primarily divided into single-mode and multi-mode types, each serving distinct applications in fiber optic networks. Single-mode connectors utilize a single light path, enabling long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunication and data center applications. In contrast, multi-mode connectors accommodate multiple light paths, facilitating shorter distance communication with higher bandwidth capabilities, often used in local area networks and video applications. Each type is critical for optimizing network performance and connectivity.</p></p>
