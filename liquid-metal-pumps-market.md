<p><ul><li>"Conduction Pumps"</li><li>"Induction Pumps"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid-metal pumps market features two main types: conduction pumps and induction pumps. Conduction pumps utilize the thermal conductivity of liquid metals to transfer heat energy, making them effective in high-temperature applications such as nuclear reactors. Conversely, induction pumps rely on electromagnetic forces to move liquid metals, offering efficient and versatile pumping solutions. Each type serves specific industrial needs, with conduction pumps excelling in thermal management while induction pumps provide flexibility in various operating conditions and are suitable for a broader range of applications.</p></p>
