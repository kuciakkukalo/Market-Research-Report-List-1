<p><p>Brown Fused Alumina is produced using two main types of furnace processes: dumping furnace and fixed furnace. The dumping furnace type involves a continuous operation where raw materials are fed and melted, allowing for efficient production with higher yields. In contrast, the fixed furnace type operates in batch mode, where the materials are processed in a stationary furnace. Both types influence the quality and characteristics of the alumina produced, impacting their suitability for different industrial applications.</p></p>
