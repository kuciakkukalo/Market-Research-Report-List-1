<p><p>PBT Neat Resin is categorized into two primary market types: Injection Grade PBT and Extrusion Grade PBT. Injection Grade PBT is designed for high-precision molding applications, ensuring enhanced dimensional stability and surface finish, making it ideal for intricate components in automotive and electronics industries. Conversely, Extrusion Grade PBT is tailored for processes involving continuous shaping, such as sheets and profiles, providing versatility in manufacturing while maintaining strength and thermal stability for diverse applications, including packaging and construction materials.</p></p>
