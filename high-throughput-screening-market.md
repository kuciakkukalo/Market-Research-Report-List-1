<p><p>High Throughput Screening (HTS) is a systematic process used in drug discovery and molecular biology to swiftly evaluate a large number of biological compounds for their activity against specific targets. Furthermore, the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning in HTS is revolutionizing data analysis and interpretation, streamlining the drug development process.
