<p><p>Multilayer varistors (MLVs) are essential components in the automotive market, offering protection against voltage spikes. SMD (Surface Mount Device) multilayer varistors are designed for compact circuit boards, allowing for high-density assembly and improved thermal performance. In contrast, through-hole multilayer varistors are mounted via drilled holes, providing robust mechanical support and suitability for larger applications. Both types effectively suppress transients, ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic systems in vehicles, making them critical for modern automotive designs.</p></p>
