<p><p>Fused quartz plates and discs are categorized into two primary market types: transparent and opaque. Transparent fused quartz exhibits clarity and optical properties ideal for applications in optics, electronics, and harsh environments, offering high thermal resistance and low expansion. In contrast, opaque fused quartz has limited light transmission and is primarily used for applications requiring high durability, such as protective barriers or components in high-temperature settings. The choice between these types depends on the specific requirements of the intended application.</p></p>
