<p><ul><li>Underground Mining</li><li>Open Pit Mining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper mining market primarily consists of two main types: underground mining and open-pit mining. Underground mining involves extracting copper from deep deposits, using tunnels and shafts, and is ideal for high-grade ores located beneath the surface. Open-pit mining, on the other hand, involves removing large amounts of earth and rock to access copper deposits closer to the surface. Each method has distinct operational costs, environmental impacts, and efficiency levels, shaping the overall dynamics of the copper mining industry.</p></p>
