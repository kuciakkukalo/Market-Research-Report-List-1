<p><p>Electromagnetic flowmeter converters are classified into two main types: integrated and remote. Integrated type converters combine the flow sensing and signal processing components within a single unit, offering streamlined installation and maintenance. They are particularly suited for applications with space constraints. In contrast, remote type converters separate the sensing and processing elements, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and enabling monitoring in challenging environments. This segmentation caters to various industrial needs based on spatial and operational requirements.</p></p>
