<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer Transfer System market encompasses two primary types: horizontal transfer and vertical transfer systems. Horizontal transfer systems move wafers in a parallel orientation, ideal for high-volume applications requiring efficient space usage and minimized contamination. In contrast, vertical transfer systems handle wafers in a vertical orientation, which enhances stability during transport and allows for compact designs. Both types are crucial for maintaining the integrity of semiconductor manufacturing processes, ensuring precise handling and minimal damage during transfer.</p></p>
