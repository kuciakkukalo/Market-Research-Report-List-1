<p><p>The removable tape market is divided into two primary types: single-sided and double-sided tape. Single-sided removable tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for temporary applications like mounting or crafting where easy removal is essential. Double-sided removable tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on both sides, offering a strong bond between two surfaces while still allowing for clean removal. Both types cater to various industries, including packaging, office supplies, and DIY projects, emphasizing versatility and convenience.</p></p>
