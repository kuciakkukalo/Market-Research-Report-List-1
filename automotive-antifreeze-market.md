<p><strong>The Automotive Antifreeze Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ethylene Glycol</li><li>Propylene Glycol</li><li>Glycerin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive antifreeze market consists of three main types: ethylene glycol, propylene glycol, and glycerin. Ethylene glycol is widely used due to its excellent freeze and boil-over protection and low cost, making it the most prevalent type. Propylene glycol, though more expensive, is considered safer for food and potable water applications, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. Glycerin, a less common choice, is biodegradable and non-toxic, often utilized in niche markets and applications where eco-friendliness is prioritized.</p></p>
