<p><p>The Rapid Absorbable Sutures market is categorized into two main types: natural absorbable sutures and synthetic absorbable sutures. Natural absorbable sutures, made from materials like catgut, are derived from biological sources and break down in the body through enzymatic processes. Synthetic absorbable sutures, made from polymers like polylactic acid, offer controlled absorption rates and enhanced strength. Both types are widely used in surgical procedures for tissue approximation and wound closure, promoting healing while minimizing the need for suture removal.</p></p>
