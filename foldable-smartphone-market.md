<p><ul><li>"Inward Fold"</li><li>"Outward Fold"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foldable smartphone market has two primary types: "Inward Fold" and "Outward Fold." Inward fold devices feature screens that fold inward, creating a compact design when closed, often resembling a traditional smartphone. This design protects the display and provides a seamless user experience. In contrast, outward fold devices have displays that fold outward, allowing for a larger screen area while maintaining a phone-like form factor. This type offers versatility but can be more vulnerable to scratches when unfolded.</p></p>
