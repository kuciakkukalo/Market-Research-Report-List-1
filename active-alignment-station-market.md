<p><ul><li>4 DOF</li><li>5 DOF</li><li>6 DOF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active alignment stations facilitate the precise alignment of optical components in various applications, classified by degrees of freedom (DOF). The 4 DOF market typically involves basic adjustments such as x, y translation and rotation around two axes, suitable for simpler setups. The 5 DOF market adds an additional rotation, accommodating more complex systems. The 6 DOF market offers full spatial control, allowing for comprehensive alignment in intricate optical assemblies. Each type caters to differing levels of precision and operational complexity required in specific industries.</p></p>
