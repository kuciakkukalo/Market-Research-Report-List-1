<p><p>The coloured contact lenses market consists of three main types: soft contact lenses, hybrid contact lenses, and rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses. </p><p>Soft contact lenses are flexible and comfortable, ideal for cosmetic enhancement and vision correction. Hybrid contact lenses combine a soft lens skirt with a rigid center, offering the comfort of soft lenses while maintaining clear vision. Rigid lenses, known for durability and sharp vision, are less flexible but provide excellent oxygen permeability, making them suitable for long-term use and vision correction.</p></p>
