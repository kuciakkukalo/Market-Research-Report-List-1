<p><strong>The IP CCTV Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Cameras</li><li>Decentralized IP Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IP CCTV camera market comprises two main types: centralized and decentralized cameras. Centralized IP cameras connect to a central network video recorder (NVR), allowing for streamlined management and storage of footage. This system is beneficial for large-scale monitoring setups, offering easier maintenance and control. In contrast, decentralized IP cameras operate independently, with each camera handling its own recording and storage, often using cloud services. This setup allows for greater flexibility and scalability, making it suitable for smaller or evolving installations.</p></p>
