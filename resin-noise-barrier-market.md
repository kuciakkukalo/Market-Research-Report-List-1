<p><ul><li>Reflective Type Noise Barrier</li><li>ABSorptive Type Noise Barrier</li><li>Mixed Type Noise Barrier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Resin Noise Barrier market comprises three main types: Reflective, Absorptive, and Mixed Type Noise Barriers. Reflective Type Noise Barriers deflect sound waves to minimize noise impact, making them effective in urban areas. Absorptive Type Noise Barriers, on the other hand, absorb sound waves, reducing noise pollution by converting sound energy into heat. Mixed Type Noise Barriers combine both reflective and absorptive properties, providing a balanced approach to noise mitigation, suitable for diverse environments and enhancing acoustic performance.</p></p>
