<p><p>Semi-automated dental laboratory ovens are categorized into several types based on their heating mechanisms and applications. Muffle ovens provide even heat distribution for materials like ceramics. Vacuum ovens remove air during the heating process, preventing oxidation for metal and ceramic materials. Infrared ovens use radiant heat for quick sintering and drying. Microwave ovens offer rapid heating, ideal for certain dental resins. Other market types may include specialized ovens designed for particular dental applications or materials, catering to diverse laboratory needs.</p></p>
