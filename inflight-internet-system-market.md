<p><strong>The Inflight Internet System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATG</li><li>Ka Band Satellite</li><li>Ku Band Satellite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inflight internet system market comprises three key types: ATG, Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG (Air-To-Ground) utilizes terrestrial towers for broadband connectivity, offering lower latency and cost-effective solutions. Ka Band Satellite offers high-speed data transmission with greater bandwidth, making it suitable for high-demand passengers but requires more complex infrastructure. Ku Band Satellite provides a balanced approach, delivering reliable service with a mix of bandwidth and coverage, making it a popular choice for airlines worldwide.</p></p>
