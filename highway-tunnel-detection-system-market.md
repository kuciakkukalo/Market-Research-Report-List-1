<p><p>The Highway Tunnel Detection System market encompasses various technologies for identifying structural anomalies in tunnels. Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) uses electromagnetic waves to detect subsurface anomalies. Ground Robots facilitate inspection through mobility and real-time data capture. Surface Seismic Systems assess tunnel integrity by measuring ground vibrations. Microgravity Detection Systems (MDS) identify changes in gravitational fields caused by voids or flaws within tunnel structures. Other market segments may include advanced imaging techniques and AI-driven diagnostic tools, enhancing detection capabilities.</p></p>
