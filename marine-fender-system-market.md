<p><p>The Marine Fender System market encompasses three main types: solid rubber fenders, pneumatic fenders, and foam fenders. Solid rubber fenders are durable and suited for high-impact scenarios, providing robust protection against naval collisions. Pneumatic fenders, which are inflatable, offer excellent energy absorption and are favored for larger vessels in open waters. Foam fenders are lightweight and resistant to certain environmental factors, making them versatile for various applications. Each type caters to specific maritime needs for safety and stability.</p></p>
