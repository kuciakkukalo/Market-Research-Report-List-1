<p><p>The Dimensional Metrology Equipment market comprises various tools for precise measurements. Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM) provide 3D measurements of physical objects. Optical Digitizers & Scanners (ODS) convert physical dimensions into digital data. Vision Measuring Machines (VMM) employ cameras for measuring objects with high accuracy. Form Measuring Machines (FMM) assess surface contours and geometrical features. Measurement Gages (MG) are used for quick checks of dimensions, while Calipers & Micrometers (C&M) offer detailed and accurate linear measurements, crucial in quality control processes.</p></p>
