<p><p>The cryogenic insulation market comprises various types, each with unique properties. Polystyrene, known for its lightweight and cost-effectiveness, is commonly used in less demanding applications. Fiberglass is valued for its durability, while perlite, a volcanic glass, offers good insulation properties and is often used in storage tanks. Other materials include aerogels and vacuum insulation panels, catering to specific needs.</p></p>
