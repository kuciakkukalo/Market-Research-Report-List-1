<p><p>The Industrial 3D Sensor market comprises several key types. "Structured Light" sensors project a series of light patterns onto objects to capture their shape and dimensions, making them ideal for high-precision measurements. "Time of Flight" (TOF) sensors measure the time it takes for emitted light to return, enabling fast depth perception and distance estimation, suitable for dynamic environments. "Others" include various technologies like LIDAR and stereo vision, catering to specific industrial applications like robotics, automation, and quality control.</p></p>
